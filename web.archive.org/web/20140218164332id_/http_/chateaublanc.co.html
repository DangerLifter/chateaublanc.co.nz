Christchurch Earthquake Update
The earthquake on 22nd February 2011 caused considerable destruction in the CBD and damage to our property. The south end of our property will be demolished. The north end, although suffering no structural damage, has significant land issues underneath with further exploratory work to be done. Any remedial work, if undertaken, will be significant and we do not anticipate re opening the hotel prior to mid 2013. Due to the uncertainty we are not taking any bookings. We will continue to provide updates here as we have more information.
